## Strategy Summary
- **Total keywords: 246** (all broad match)
- **Total negative keywords: 201**
- **Match type: ALL broad match** — chosen because Ridgecrest's luxury remodeling market in the East Bay is small. Exact/phrase match resulted in zero impressions on most keywords. Broad match with geo-targeting (17 zip codes, PRESENCE only) and 201 negatives provides reach with protection.
- **Replaces: 45 original keywords** (20 broad, 19 phrase, 6 exact) — most had zero impressions because phrase/exact match on luxury-modified terms like "luxury home remodel East Bay" don't match how people actually search.

## Why Original 45 Keywords Failed
- 19 phrase match keywords used luxury/high-end modifiers + "East Bay" or city names — nobody types "luxury home remodel East Bay" into Google
- 6 exact match keywords had the same problem — near-zero search volume for those exact phrases
- Keywords were built based on how Ridgecrest describes itself, not how homeowners search
- Only "home remodel contractor" (broad match) was driving clicks

## What Was Intentionally LEFT OUT of Negatives
- "free estimate" / "free consultation" — Ridgecrest offers a free discovery call
- "review" / "reviews" — "Ridgecrest Designs reviews" is a hot lead
- "best" / "top" — "best remodeling contractor Walnut Creek" is target traffic
- "cost" / "price" / "how much" — cost searchers are closer to buying than idea searchers (ClicksGeek, April 2026)
- Competitor names (Asher Brothers, Gordon Reese, Benchmark) — their brand searches capture people shopping for luxury remodeling
