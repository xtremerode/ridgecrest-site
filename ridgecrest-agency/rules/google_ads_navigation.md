# Google Ads UI Navigation Workflows
## Updated: April 11, 2026

---

## Auction Insights
1. Click **Campaigns** in left sidebar
2. Click on **Perplexity Test One** to go inside it
3. Click the **Keywords** tab at the top
4. Click **Search keywords**
5. Check the box at the top left to select all keywords
6. A blue action bar appears — click **Auction insights** in that bar

## Asset Studio (Image Management)
1. Click **Tools** in the left sidebar (wrench icon)
2. Search for or find **Asset Studio**
3. Click **Image** tab to filter to images only
4. Select images → Remove or Upload from this screen

## Assets (Campaign-Level)
1. Click **Campaigns** in left sidebar → expand submenu
2. Click **Assets** → **Assets**
3. Use tabs at top to filter: All, Image, Sitelink, Callout, etc.
4. Filters bar shows: Asset status, Asset type, +1 more

## Campaign Settings
1. Click **Campaigns** in left sidebar
2. Click on **Perplexity Test One**
3. Click **Campaign settings** link in top right (next to Optimization score)

## Ad Groups
1. Click **Campaigns** in left sidebar
2. Click on **Perplexity Test One**
3. Ad groups table is the default view

## Keywords
1. Click **Campaigns** in left sidebar
2. Click on **Perplexity Test One**
3. Click **Keywords** tab at top
4. Click **Search keywords** to see all keywords

## Search Terms Report
1. Click **Campaigns** in left sidebar
2. Click on **Perplexity Test One**
3. Click **Keywords** tab at top
4. Click **Search terms** (next to Search keywords)

## Google Ads Scripts
1. Click **Tools** in left sidebar
2. Click **Scripts** under Bulk Actions
3. Click **+** to create new or click existing script name to edit
4. Click **Preview** to test, **Run** to execute

## Event Subscriptions (Slack App)
1. Go to https://api.slack.com/apps/A0APF6R39JL
2. Click **Event Subscriptions** in left sidebar under Features
3. Add/remove bot events here
4. After changes, reinstall app from **OAuth & Permissions** page

## OAuth & Permissions (Slack App)
1. Go to https://api.slack.com/apps/A0APF6R39JL/oauth?
2. Scroll to **Bot Token Scopes**
3. Add scopes here
4. Reinstall app after adding scopes
