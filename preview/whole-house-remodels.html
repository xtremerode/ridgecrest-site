Our Process

Four stages. Complete transformation.
Managed entirely by us.

1
Design

Full architectural scope from day one

We begin with a complete assessment of your home's existing structure, systems, and layout — then develop a full architectural plan that addresses every space. Floor plans, elevations, structural drawings, and material specifications are developed before any permit is submitted or any demo begins.

2
Render

Every room rendered in precise detail

Photo-realistic 3D renders are produced for each key space in your home. You'll see every finish, fixture, cabinet, and material choice visualized with photographic accuracy. This eliminates guesswork, prevents change orders, and gives you the confidence to make decisions before construction begins.

3
Permit

Structural and code compliance managed in-house

Whole-house remodels typically require multiple permit types — structural, mechanical, electrical, plumbing. Our team prepares and manages all documentation, coordinates with local jurisdictions and inspectors, and resolves code questions before they become construction delays. This is a capability most design firms don't have in-house.

4
Build

Trade specialists, coordinated seamlessly

Our construction team manages every trade — framing, MEP, tile, finish carpentry, millwork, painting, and more — in the correct sequence, with no handoff gaps. Your dedicated project manager is on-site throughout. You receive weekly progress updates, milestone sign-offs, and a final walkthrough before we consider the project complete.
