Our Process

Designed before demolition.
Built without compromise.

1
Design

Custom layout built around your kitchen life

We begin by understanding how your household uses the kitchen — cooking patterns, entertaining style, storage requirements, appliance preferences. From there, our design team develops a custom cabinet layout, island configuration, material palette, and appliance specification tailored to how you actually live, not a template.

2
Render

See every finish before we order a single cabinet

Photo-realistic 3D renders show your kitchen exactly as it will look — stone slab veining, cabinet door profiles, hardware finishes, lighting fixtures, flooring, and all. You'll make every material decision with full visual context, not swatches and imagination. Revisions happen in the render, not in the field.

3
Permit

Plumbing, electrical, and structural handled

High-specification kitchens often involve plumbing relocations, electrical panel upgrades, and structural modifications. Our team prepares all permit documentation, coordinates with local inspectors, and manages the approval timeline so construction starts on schedule — not after months of back-and-forth.

4
Build

Specialists in cabinetry, stone, and finish work

Custom cabinet installation, stone fabrication and templating, tile setting, and appliance integration are performed by trade specialists who work exclusively on high-specification projects. Your project manager coordinates every phase, ensures quality at each milestone, and delivers a kitchen that performs as precisely as it looks.
