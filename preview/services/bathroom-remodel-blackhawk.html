Bathroom Remodel · Blackhawk, CA

Built for Blackhawk.
Built without compromise.

A master bathroom is one of the most personal spaces in a home — and in Blackhawk, where the architecture and finish level of the homes themselves sets a high bar, the bathroom needs to match. Ridgecrest Designs designs and builds bathrooms that function as spa-level retreats: large-format tile work, custom vanity millwork, radiant floor heating, steam showers, and lighting that flatters rather than glares.

Blackhawk is one of the most exclusive planned residential communities in Northern California — six gated neighborhoods surrounding two Blackhawk Country Club golf courses, with a median home price above $2 million and estates ranging to well over $8 million. The design standards in Blackhawk are enforced by an HOA with real authority, and every project must meet both community architectural standards and Contra Costa County permitting requirements. Working here requires a firm accustomed to operating at the top of the market.

Every bathroom project begins with photo-realistic 3D renders of the finished space — tile layout confirmed at scale, fixtures positioned precisely, material combinations verified for coherence. By the time construction begins, every decision has already been made. There are no field-level substitutions or on-the-fly design choices.

Scope of Work

What's included in a
bathroom remodel with Ridgecrest

Every Ridgecrest bathroom project includes full design-build management, photo-realistic renders before construction, and complete permit and inspection management. One firm handles everything from first design meeting to final walkthrough.

Bathroom remodel projects with Ridgecrest Designs start at $60,000 for a guest bath and $100,000 for a master bathroom. High-end master suites in Blackhawk typically run $150,000 to $250,000 depending on size and specification.

  • Full design consultation and space planning
  • Photo-realistic 3D renders of the finished bathroom
  • Tile selection and layout planning — floor and wall
  • Custom vanity and millwork design
  • Fixture specification — faucets, shower systems, tub
  • Lighting design including shower and vanity lighting
  • Radiant floor heating design and installation
  • Plumbing reconfiguration and drain relocation where needed
  • Permit management through final inspection
  • Active project management through completion

What makes us different

Tile Layout Precision

Tile work in a luxury bathroom requires planning at the real scale of the room, not a rendering approximation. We lay out every tile pattern in our renders so you approve the exact result before grout is ever mixed.

Photo-Realistic Renders

See your finished bathroom in photographic detail before any demolition begins. Every finish confirmed. Every material approved. No surprises at completion.

Blackhawk HOA Compliance

We manage HOA design review and County permits simultaneously, keeping your project moving on both approval tracks without requiring homeowner involvement in the process.

Custom Millwork

We design custom vanity millwork to the exact dimensions of your space — not catalog cabinets that almost fit. Every inch of storage is intentional.

Systems Expertise

Radiant floor heating, steam shower systems, and multi-outlet shower controls require careful planning of electrical load, drain sizing, and waterproofing. We design all of it before the walls open up.
