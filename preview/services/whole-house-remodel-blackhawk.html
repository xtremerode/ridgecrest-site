Whole House Remodel · Blackhawk, CA

Built for Blackhawk.
Built without compromise.

A whole house remodel is the most complex residential project a homeowner undertakes — and the most consequential. Done right, it transforms a home that wasn't quite working into one that functions exactly as the family needs it to. Done wrong, it is an expensive lesson in why integration matters. Ridgecrest Designs manages whole house remodels as fully integrated design-build projects: every space planned simultaneously, every system coordinated, every finish cohesive.

Blackhawk is one of the most exclusive planned residential communities in Northern California — six gated neighborhoods surrounding two Blackhawk Country Club golf courses, with a median home price above $2 million and estates ranging to well over $8 million. The design standards in Blackhawk are enforced by an HOA with real authority, and every project must meet both community architectural standards and Contra Costa County permitting requirements. Working here requires a firm accustomed to operating at the top of the market.

Our whole house remodels begin with a comprehensive design phase — all rooms rendered in photo-realistic 3D before a single permit is filed. We present the complete vision first, then sequence the construction to minimize disruption and maximize efficiency. For projects of this scale, living in the home during construction is sometimes possible and sometimes not — we assess that honestly at the start, not mid-project.

Scope of Work

What's included in a
whole house remodel with Ridgecrest

Every whole house remodel begins with a full scope assessment — evaluating the structural condition, the mechanical systems, and the program of spaces the family needs. We establish the full scope before design begins so there are no mid-project additions that blow the budget.

Whole house remodels with Ridgecrest Designs start at $1,000,000 for comprehensive scope. Projects in Blackhawk's estate properties often range from $1.5 million to $4 million depending on square footage, structural scope, and finish specification.

  • Full scope assessment and whole-home program development
  • Photo-realistic 3D renders of every major space
  • Structural analysis and load-bearing assessment
  • Phased construction sequencing plan
  • Kitchen design and remodel
  • Master bath and secondary bath remodel
  • Living, dining, and bedroom renovation
  • Complete HVAC, electrical, and plumbing modernization
  • Flooring, cabinetry, and finish specification throughout
  • Full permit management and inspection coordination

Full-Home Vision Before Construction

We render every major space in photographic detail before any permit is filed. The kitchen, master bath, living areas, and bedrooms — all coordinated into a cohesive whole before a wall comes down.

Systems Modernization

A whole house remodel is the only opportunity to address the entire mechanical, electrical, and plumbing infrastructure at once. We evaluate all three and design upgrades that serve the home for the next 30 years.

Blackhawk HOA Management

Whole house remodels in Blackhawk require HOA design review for exterior modifications. We manage the community approval process and the County permit process simultaneously.

Construction Phasing

The sequence of a whole house remodel directly affects timeline, cost, and your ability to occupy parts of the home during construction. We plan that sequence before construction begins and stick to it.
