Our Process

Four stages. One team.
Zero gaps.

1
Design

Architecture built around how you live

We begin with a deep consultation to understand your vision, your site, and how your family uses space. Our architectural team then develops custom floor plans, elevations, and structural drawings — designed specifically for your land, your lifestyle, and the surrounding community.

2
Render

See your home before we build it

Before a single permit is filed, you'll see your home in photo-realistic 3D detail. Every finish, fixture, and material choice is visualized with precision. We refine until the render matches your expectations exactly — then we lock it in. What you see is what we build.

3
Permit

California permitting managed end-to-end

Our in-house team prepares and submits all permit documentation, manages agency responses, and handles structural and engineering sign-off. We know California residential code, local jurisdictions, and HOA approval processes — eliminating delays that catch other builders off guard.

4
Build

Expert construction with active oversight

Construction begins with your dedicated project manager on-site throughout. We work with specialized trade teams whose work is measured by long-term performance, not just delivery-day appearance. You receive regular updates, milestone walkthroughs, and a final delivery that matches the render you approved.
