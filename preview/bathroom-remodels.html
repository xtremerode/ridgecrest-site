Our Process

Designed with precision.
Built by specialists.

1
Design

Space planning and material selection done first

We begin with your goals for the space — how you use it, what you want it to feel like, and what standards it needs to meet. Custom vanity design, tile layout, shower and tub configuration, lighting plan, and all fixture and hardware selections are developed before any demolition begins. Every decision is made on paper first.

2
Render

See your finished bathroom before demo starts

A photo-realistic 3D render shows you your bathroom exactly as it will look — tile patterns, grout lines, fixture finishes, vanity profiles, mirror placement, and lighting. You make every visual decision with full clarity. There are no surprises mid-construction, no expensive changes once work is underway.

3
Permit

Waterproofing, plumbing, and electrical signed off

Bathroom renovations require plumbing permits, electrical permits, and in many cases structural review. Our team prepares and submits all documentation, schedules inspections, and ensures that waterproofing and mechanical systems are permitted and inspected at the right phases — protecting your investment long term.

4
Build

Tile setters, plumbers, and finish carpenters in sequence

Bathroom construction requires precise sequencing — waterproofing before tile, rough plumbing before walls close, steam and heated floor systems before substrate goes down. Our project manager coordinates every trade in the correct order, maintains quality standards at each phase, and delivers a space where every detail was done once, done right.
